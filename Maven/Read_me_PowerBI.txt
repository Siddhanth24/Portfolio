This project showcases an interactive dashboard created using Power BI to analyze marketing data and provide valuable insights for Mavin Market. The dashboard offers a comprehensive view of key marketing metrics, enabling data-driven decision-making and improved marketing strategies.

Key Features
Interactive Visualizations: The dashboard includes a variety of interactive charts, graphs, and visualizations that allow users to explore and analyze marketing data in a dynamic and engaging way.

KPI Tracking: Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) such as conversion rate, customer acquisition cost, and revenue are prominently displayed, providing a quick overview of the marketing performance and goals.

Data Filters: Users can easily filter and slice the data based on various criteria such as date range, campaign type, or target audience. This feature allows for a more granular analysis and customized insights.

Data Modeling: The project involved creating a robust data model to efficiently organize and structure the marketing data. This ensures accurate and reliable reporting and analysis.
