Description
The purpose of this project is to perform data cleaning on secondary data related to employee absences during normal working hours. The focus is on preserving high productivity rates in a company by ensuring that the raw CSV data is converted into meaningful and accurate information.

Goals:
Clean the data by removing mistakes, missing values, and incomprehensible or irrelevant entries.

Provide a clean and reliable dataset that can be used for further analysis and insights.

Improve data quality and ensure that only accurate and meaningful information is utilized.

Dataset Information:
Dataset Name: Absenteeism-data.csv
Description: The dataset consists of various attributes related to employee absences, such as reasons for absence, transportation expense, distance to work, age, education level, and more.
